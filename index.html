Why Private Real Estate

A Complement to
the Public
Market Sleeve

Private real estate provides return streams that are structurally differentiated from public equities and fixed income — with income generation, inflation sensitivity, and reduced daily volatility as core attributes of a well-constructed allocation.

Access to these strategies is typically institutional. Assurican's positioning within the Equiton platform enables qualified clients to participate in strategies not available through traditional bank-managed portfolios.

Private real estate investments are less liquid than publicly traded securities and are only suitable where the client's objectives, time horizon, and liquidity profile support the allocation. All allocations are documented within the client's Investment Policy Statement and subject to ongoing suitability review.
